About us page

Affluent Financial Services (AFS) is a Certified Public Accounting firm based in Rockville, Maryland. Affluent Financial Services LLC (AFS) is a professional services firm providing audit and assurance, tax, accounting, financial advisory and consulting services to a wide range of not-for-profit tax-exempt organizations and privately held companies. AFS provides professional services to clients in the Washington D.C., Virginia and Maryland. Not-for-profits is our niche industry sector that makes more than 50% of our client portfolio. We believe in gaining trust and build strong & long term relationship with our clients. Our core values include honesty and integrity, professionalism, competence, dedication, responsibility and accountability, which not only helped our firm grow, it helped our clients with better understanding of technical issues, develop effective operational controls, and efficient processes to administer their programs and budgets and to meet their internal and external compliance and other requirements..
